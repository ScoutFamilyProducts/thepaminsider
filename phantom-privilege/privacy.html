Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2026

The PAM Insider does not sell your data.
We do not retain organizational decisions or security stack choices — even in simulation.
What happens in your session stays on your device.

1. Who We Are

The PAM Insider is the publisher of Phantom Privilege, a cybersecurity educational experience. We operate independently and can be reached at thepaminsider@gmail.com and thepaminsider.com.

2. What We Collect and Why

2.1 Anonymous Device Identifier

When you first launch Phantom Privilege, the app generates a random unique identifier (UUID) stored locally on your device. This identifier is randomly generated — not derived from your name, email, or any personal information. It is used to associate anonymous usage events with a consistent device across sessions and is never transmitted to any third party. It can be reset by uninstalling and reinstalling the app.

2.2 License Redemption Data

When you redeem a license code, we store the following on our backend server:

  • The license code redeemed
  • Your anonymous device identifier
  • The timestamp of redemption
  • The game profile selected at time of redemption

We do not collect your name, email address, or payment information during license redemption.

2.3 Anonymous Usage Events

If you have redeemed a license, the app may transmit anonymous usage events to our backend. These include game completion data, scenario outcomes, whether the Phantom Privilege scenario was triggered, and certificate generation events. No personally identifiable information is included.

2.4 Email Address (Optional)

If you voluntarily submit your email through the teaser page or any in-app notification signup, we store that address solely to notify you of product updates on launch day. One email. Nothing else. You may request removal at any time by contacting thepaminsider@gmail.com.

2.5 Head-to-Head Session Data

If you participate in a head-to-head multiplayer session, temporary session data (room codes, tokens, turn actions, game state) is stored on our backend server. This data does not include your security stack decisions or organizational information — those exist only on your device. Session data is automatically deleted within 24 hours of room creation. The session debrief is generated entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2.6 What We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, postal address, phone number, or government-issued identifier
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Browsing history or data from other apps
  • Payment card or banking information
  • Your in-game security stack decisions, vendor selections, or organizational strategy

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

Data collected by Phantom Privilege is stored on backend servers hosted through Railway (railway.app), a cloud infrastructure provider based in the United States. All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. Admin-accessible endpoints are protected by secret key authentication. Persistent storage is isolated to our dedicated server volume. Room session data is automatically purged on a scheduled cleanup cycle.

4. Data Retention

  • Anonymous device identifier and license redemption records: retained for the life of the license
  • Anonymous usage events: retained indefinitely in aggregate for product improvement
  • Email addresses: retained until you request removal
  • Head-to-head session data: automatically deleted within 24 hours of room creation
  • Session debrief content: never transmitted — exists only on your device

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • With enterprise license administrators: aggregate anonymous usage reports only — no individual user data
  • With service providers: our backend infrastructure provider (Railway) processes data on our behalf
  • As required by law: if compelled by valid legal process

6. Children's Privacy

Phantom Privilege is designed for professional and educational use by adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information, contact us at thepaminsider@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to know what data we hold, request correction or deletion, and opt out of email communications. Contact thepaminsider@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. Because most data we collect is anonymous, some requests may be limited by our technical ability to locate the relevant records.

8. Third-Party Platforms

Phantom Privilege is distributed through the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Those platforms have their own privacy policies governing how they handle data related to your use of their services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party platforms.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Your continued use of Phantom Privilege after any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
