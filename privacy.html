Privacy Policy

No social. No accounts. No tracking.

Overview

The PAM Field Guide is a free, offline-first mobile application published by The PAM Insider. This privacy policy describes how the app handles your information — which is to say, it doesn't collect any.

This policy applies to The PAM Field Guide mobile application and the thepaminsider.com website.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal information of any kind
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage data or behavioral analytics
  • Crash reports sent to external servers
  • Email addresses or account information
  • Payment information
  • Any data that leaves your device

What Stays on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only, using standard device storage. This data is never transmitted anywhere:

  • Terms you have saved as favorites
  • Scenarios you have marked as completed
  • Whether you have completed the onboarding screens

This data exists only on your device. It is not synced, backed up to any server, or accessible by The PAM Insider or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

No Analytics or Tracking

The PAM Field Guide does not include any analytics SDK, tracking library, advertising framework, or behavioral profiling tool. There are no third-party trackers in the app.

No Accounts Required

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use any feature. All content is available immediately with no registration.

Feedback

If you choose to send feedback using the in-app feedback button, it opens your device's email application with a pre-filled template addressed to thepaminsider@gmail.com. No data is submitted automatically. You choose what to write and whether to send it. The PAM Insider receives only what you choose to include in that email.

Please do not include passwords, internal system details, customer names, or sensitive security information in feedback emails.

Optional Support / Donations

If you choose to support the project via the Buy Me a Coffee link, you are directed to buymeacoffee.com, which is an independent third-party service with its own privacy policy and terms. The PAM Insider does not receive or store any payment or personal information from that transaction.

Website

The thepaminsider.com website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. It does not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics, or any form of visitor identification. No personal data is collected through the website.

Children's Privacy

The PAM Field Guide is designed for adult professionals working in or learning about cybersecurity and IT. The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Given that we collect no data, substantive changes to this policy are unlikely.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at thepaminsider@gmail.com
