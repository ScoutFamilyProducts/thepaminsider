The Idea

A tool practitioners keep asking for

Security practitioners are being pulled into compliance conversations they do not always feel equipped for. Auditors ask about PAM controls. Cyber insurance underwriters ask about privileged access. Board-level risk conversations reference framework alignment. The people who actually run the security program need a fast, honest way to understand where they stand — not a vendor assessment, not a consultant engagement.

The PAM Compliance Mapper is being designed to fill that gap. Answer a set of practical questions about your environment and get a clear mapping against the frameworks that matter.
